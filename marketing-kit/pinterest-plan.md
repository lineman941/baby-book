## Rules

- 3–4 pins per week, spread out — Pinterest rewards consistency over bursts.
- Re-pin your best performer to a second board after 2 weeks.
- Don't change the pin link after posting (resets its ranking).
- Pin titles are search terms — never cutesy-only. "Digital Baby Memory Book" beats "So Precious!".
