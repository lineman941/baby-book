Every video is a **screen recording of the app** (or phone-in-hand shot) — no face needed. 15–30 seconds. Post 1/day or every other day, 7–9pm. Same link-in-bio rule as the week-1 plan: never say "buy", say "link in bio".

**Core hashtags (pick 3 + 2 rotating):** #babyjournal #babymilestones #digitaldownload #newmom #firsttimemom #babybook #momtok #babyshowergift #pregnancyjournal #newborn

**How to record:** open the app (your own copy or the demo link), start iPhone/Android screen recording, follow the "Show" column, stop, trim, add trending audio + captions.
