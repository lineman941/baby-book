## Why this order works

TikTok/Reels = fast reach spikes; Pinterest = slow compounding traffic (baby niche is one of Pinterest's biggest — pins keep driving clicks for months). Post TikTok 7–9pm, Pinterest anytime.

## Rules for every post

- Reply to every comment in the first hour (algorithm boost)
- Same 3–5 core hashtags: #babyjournal #babymilestones #digitaldownload + 2 rotating
- Never say "buy" — say "link in bio" or "I put the link in my bio"
