## What the business is

A digital baby memory book sold as a one-time-purchase file.

- **Product:** `dist/OurLittleMiracle.html` — self-contained web app (built by `build-product.py`); buyers get it + `sales-kit/welcome-guide.pdf` via instant download.
- **Storefront/demo (free hosting):** https://lineman941.github.io/baby-book/ (GitHub Pages; landing page + free demo at `/app.html`).
- **Sales channel (Phase 1):** Payhip — $0 upfront, 5% per sale. Listing copy: `sales-kit/payhip-listing.md`.
- **Sales channel (Phase 2, at ~$30 revenue):** Etsy — `sales-kit/etsy-listing.md` + `etsy-dashboard.html` (API control panel).
- **Traffic:** organic Pinterest / TikTok / communities — playbooks in `marketing-kit/`.
