Our Little Miracle

Welcome! Your baby book is ready 💛

Everything you need to know, in 60 seconds.

1
Save the file somewhere safe
You received OurLittleMiracle.html — that file IS your baby book. Save it to your phone's Files app, your computer, or cloud storage. You can copy it anywhere; it always works.
2
Open it in your browser
Tap the file and choose Safari or Chrome. No app, no account, no internet needed. Tip: use your browser's "Add to Home Screen" so it opens like an app.
3
Start writing — it saves itself
Enter your baby's name on the cover and go. Every word and photo saves automatically on your device — 100% private, nothing is uploaded anywhere.
4
Add photos from your camera roll
Tap any photo box to add a picture — birth photo, every milestone, and one for each month of the first year.
5
Back it up (once a month is perfect)
Tap the ⚙ gear → Backup My Book. It downloads one small file with everything — words and photos. Keep it in your cloud storage or email it to yourself. To move to a new phone: open the book there and tap Restore from Backup.
6
Print it whenever you like
Tap ⚙ gear → Print / Save as PDF for a beautiful printable keepsake of every page.
Good to know
  • Your book lives in the browser you first opened it in — keep using the same one (e.g. always Safari).
  • Clearing your browser's website data can erase the book — that's what backups are for.
  • Switch color themes and fonts anytime under the ⚙ gear — your words stay put.
