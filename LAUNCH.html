Part 1 — Turn On Your Free Website (5 min, one time)

1
Make the repo public
GitHub → lineman941/baby-book → Settings → General → Danger Zone → Change visibility → Public. (Free GitHub Pages requires a public repo.)
2
Enable GitHub Pages
Settings → Pages → under "Build and deployment" set Source to GitHub Actions. After the next push to main, your site goes live at
https://lineman941.github.io/baby-book/
3
Check both pages work
Landing page: …/baby-book/ · Free demo: …/baby-book/app.html. Open them on your phone.

Part 2 — Create Your Free Store (15 min, one time)

4
Sign up at Payhip (free)
Go to payhip.com → Sign up with your email. When asked for your store link, try to claim payhip.com/OurLittleMiracle — the buy buttons on your landing page already point there. (If taken, pick another and update the links — see step 8.)
5
Connect your payout
Payhip → Settings → Payment details → connect PayPal or Stripe (both free to create). This is where your money arrives.
6
Create the product listing
Products → Add New Product → Digital Product.
• Upload dist/OurLittleMiracle.html and sales-kit/welcome-guide.pdf
• Copy the name, price ($14.97) and description from sales-kit/payhip-listing.md
• Upload the 5 images from sales-kit/images/
7
Publish and test-buy
Publish the listing, then use Payhip's preview/test checkout to confirm the download arrives by email. Open the downloaded file on your phone — type something, close, reopen: it should still be there.
8
Wire the buy buttons only if your store link differs
If you couldn't claim payhip.com/OurLittleMiracle: edit index.html on GitHub (pencil icon), find-and-replace https://payhip.com/OurLittleMiracle with your real listing URL, commit to main.
