Launch Steps — Do These in Order

1
Open the Etsy Dashboard
Go to etsy-dashboard.html. This is your launch control panel. Everything is pre-filled — title, description, tags, and pricing.
2
Connect Your Etsy Account
Click Auth & Settings in the sidebar → tap 🔗 Connect Etsy Account. A popup opens, log in to Etsy and approve. You'll be redirected back automatically. Required
3
Review Your Listing
Go to Review & Approve. Check the title, price ($4.97 launch price), and description. Make any edits you want — the live preview updates as you type.
4
Post to Etsy as DRAFT
Hit Post to Etsy. It goes up as a DRAFT (not live yet) so you can log into Etsy, add photos, and double-check before publishing. Safe
5
Add Mockup Photos on Etsy
Log into your Etsy shop and open the draft listing. Upload these 4 mockup images in this order:

① mockup_nursery_phone.png — hero shot
② mockup_flatlay_marble.png — lifestyle
③ mockup_mom_3am.png — emotional hook
④ mockup_two_screens.png — feature showcase
6
Publish the Listing
Once photos are added, click Publish on Etsy. You're live. 🎉

Pricing Schedule Auto from Dashboard

After launch, go to the Price Schedule tab in the Etsy Dashboard to update prices with one tap — no need to log into Etsy manually.
